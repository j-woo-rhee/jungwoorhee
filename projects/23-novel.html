Uncovered web novel reader behaviors for deep-personalization and author-reader connection

Timeline 2023.04 - 2023.08
Team 5 UX designers
Role in-depth research
end-to-end design

This project focused on understanding web novel reader behaviors to create a more personalized reading experience and strengthen the connection between authors and readers.

Through in-depth user research, we identified key patterns in how readers interact with web novels and developed design solutions that enhance the reading experience while providing valuable feedback to authors.
