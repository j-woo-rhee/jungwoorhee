Hi, I'm Jungwoo. I'm drawn to building interactive systems that help people capture, clarify, and work with the references they naturally make while thinking aloud — “this part,” “what I said earlier,” “it.” I'm currently a first-year PhD student in Industrial Design (HCI) at KAIST, advised by Prof. Andrea Bianchi. Before this, I earned an MSc in Industrial Design from KAIST and a BFA in Spatial Design with a Minor in Software from Kookmin University.
