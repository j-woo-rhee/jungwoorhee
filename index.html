Hi, I'm Jungwoo. I'm currently a first-year PhD student in Industrial Design (HCI) at KAIST, advised by Prof. Andrea Bianchi. Before this, I earned an MSc in Industrial Design from KAIST and a BFA in Spatial Design with a Minor in Software from Kookmin University. I'm drawn to building tools that help people think — turning messy, half-formed ideas into something they can see, shape, and make sense of.
